1. Introduction

Welcome to Split Voyage – Smart Travel Expense Tracker ("App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy describes how Split Voyage collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or our practices, please contact us at the email address provided at the end of this document.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Email address and account identifier (via Google Login or Sign in with Apple)
  • Profile name (if provided by your authentication provider)

Receipt & Expense Data

  • Receipt images uploaded by you for scanning purposes
  • Extracted receipt data (store name, date, amount, currency)
  • Trip expense records and budgets you create
  • Group expense data shared with trip members

Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage logs and crash reports for service improvement
  • AI processing inputs and outputs from receipt analysis

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account securely.
  • AI Receipt Scanning: Uploaded receipt images are processed by AI (Google Gemini API) solely to extract expense data such as store name, date, and amount. Images are used only for this extraction purpose and are not used for advertising or profiling.
  • Expense Tracking & Management: To store and organize your trip expenses, budgets, and currency conversions.
  • Group Expense Sharing: To enable collaborative expense tracking and settlement with your trip members.
  • Data Export: To generate and export your expense reports in your preferred format.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve app performance and reliability.
Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Split Voyage integrates the following third-party services to deliver its functionality:

  • Google (OAuth): Used for account authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple): Used for account authentication. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API: Used to perform AI-based analysis of receipt images for data extraction. Receipt images may be transmitted to Google's servers for processing in accordance with Google's API data usage policies.
  • Supabase: Used as our backend infrastructure for database storage, file storage, and authentication services. Data is stored on Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure.

Each third-party service is subject to its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security monitoring and updates

While we strive to protect your information using commercially acceptable means, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with our services. Specific retention practices include:

  • Receipt images: Stored until you delete them or close your account.
  • Expense and trip records: Retained while your account is active.
  • Account data: Deleted within a reasonable period following account deletion.
  • Usage logs: Retained for a limited period for operational and diagnostic purposes.

7. User Rights and Data Deletion

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and account ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights or to request account and data deletion, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

You may also delete your account directly within the App settings, which will initiate deletion of your associated data.

8. Children's Privacy

Split Voyage is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information and terminate the relevant account.

수집된 정보는 다음 목적으로 이용됩니다:

  • 계정 인증: 귀하의 신원을 확인하고 계정을 안전하게 관리하기 위해.

  • 지출 내역 관리: 여행 지출, 예산 및 환율 환산 내역을 저장하고 정리하기 위해.
  • 그룹 지출 공유: 여행 멤버들과 협력적인 지출 추적 및 정산을 가능하게 하기 위해.

  • 서비스 개선: 사용 패턴을 분석하고 버그를 수정하며 앱 성능 및 안정성을 향상시키기 위해.
중요: 운영자는 귀하의 개인정보를 상업적 또는 마케팅 목적으로 제3자에게 판매, 임대 또는 거래하지 않습니다.
