Features

Everything you need
for travel expenses

No complicated setups. Just scan, review, and move on with your trip.

Capture or Upload Receipts
Take a photo right after paying, or upload an image from your library. Receipts are stored alongside their extracted details.
Review & Edit Details
Check the extracted amount, date, and category. Make corrections easily before saving — so your records stay accurate.
Organize by Trip
Group expenses under each trip so you always know how much you spent in Tokyo, Paris, or anywhere else.
Shared Travel Spending
Track who paid what and manage costs with your travel companions — all in one place, without the mental math.
Receipt Image Storage
Every receipt image is kept with its record. Refer back to the original anytime — useful for reimbursements or expense reports.
