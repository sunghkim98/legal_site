Features

Everything you need
for travel expenses

No complicated setups. Scan, review, and move on with your trip.

Capture or Upload Receipts
Take a photo right after paying, or upload from your library. Receipts are stored with their extracted details.
Review & Edit Details
Check the extracted amount, date, and category. Make corrections easily before saving.
Organize by Trip
Group expenses under each trip — always know how much you spent in Tokyo, Paris, or anywhere else.
Shared Travel Spending
Track who paid what and split costs with your travel companions — all in one place, without the mental math.
Receipt Image Storage
Every receipt image is kept alongside its record. Refer back to the original anytime.
